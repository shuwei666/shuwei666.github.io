Ph.D. in Imaging Science, the Hong Kong Polytechnic University.

I am a Ph.D. candidate supervised by Prof. Minchen(Tommy) Wei. My research interests are image processing and computational color. Currently, I am focusing on the following topics:

  • Color constancy in challenging scenarios, such as pure color scenes, cross-sensor applications, and multi-illumination scenes.
  • AI ISP.
  • Scene relighting in shadowed and specular scenes.

See also my GitHub, 知乎 and Google Scholar. Contact me via shuwei.yue(at)connect.polyu.hk.
